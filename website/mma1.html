Multimedia - Assignment 1

Assignment Overview

In this assignment, I focused on designing and planning a multimedia product. I analysed the brief and target audience, researched similar products for inspiration, and produced design documents to show how the final multimedia product would look and work. This was a planning-only stage — I did not create the full product yet.

What I Did

I started by researching multimedia products similar to the one I was going to create. Then, I defined the purpose of my product and identified who the target audience would be. I produced a mood board, sketched storyboards, and created a design specification to show the planned look and content of the product. I also thought about how to make it suitable and engaging for the intended audience.

Skills Gained

I developed skills in:

  • Analysing briefs and identifying key requirements
  • Researching similar multimedia products
  • Producing mood boards and storyboards
  • Planning layout, colours, fonts, and content
  • Considering accessibility and usability in design
  • Communicating design ideas clearly through documentation

Website Development - Assignment 1

Assignment Overview

In this assignment, we focused on the design stage of a website project. We planned a website by researching existing designs, creating a purpose and target audience, and then designing page layouts using designs or mockups. No coding was required at this stage.

What I Did

I started by identifying the purpose of my website and who it was for. I looked at similar websites to gather inspiration and understand best practices. Then I created a mood board and sketched out designs for the homepage and other key pages. I thought carefully about layout, colours, fonts, and navigation to make sure the site would be clear and easy to use.

Skills Gained

I learned how to:

  • Plan a website for a specific purpose and audience
  • create clear designs and layouts
  • Choose colour schemes that match the theme
  • Consider accessibility and user experience
  • Explain design decisions and get feedback on my ideas
